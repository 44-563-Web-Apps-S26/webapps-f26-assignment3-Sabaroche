Juvenile Serpent and Jagger

Team Description

Juvenile Serpent is a young, agile, strong and fearless hero. She can avoid almost every attack, possesses incredible strength and endurance.
Jagger is the smart sidekick who helps with plans and keeps the hero focused during danger, whilst being able to move between dimensions.
