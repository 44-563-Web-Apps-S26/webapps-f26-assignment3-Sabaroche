Tuna Paste

To make tuna paste you need to follow this order:

  1. Open a can of tuna
  2. Drain the liquid
  3. Place the tuna in a bowl
  4. Mix in a small amount of mayonnaise
  5. Add a pinch of salt and pepper
  6. Optional ingredients to add:
    1. Chopped onions
    2. Chopped celery
    3. Red Pepper Flakes
  7. Stir until well combined
