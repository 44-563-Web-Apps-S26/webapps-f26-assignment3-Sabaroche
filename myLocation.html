My favorite place to visit is Emerald Pool, Dominica. It is an area within the Morne Trois Pitons National Park. It features a 40ft cascade with crystal clear water. It is a chilly yet quite refreshing swim. That, plus the lush green vegetation in the area, makes it quite a relaxing and enjoyable place to be.
